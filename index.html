Game Development & Direction Consultancy

I'm Sitara Shefta, a video game development consultant with expertise in project management and creative direction. I will help you to deliver high-quality, innovative games on time and within budget.

In my role as a Studio Director, I'm focused and passionate about creating and growing a thriving, ethical, inspiring culture and environment, all whilst driving Creative to make fun and quality games, and continually contributing to the growth of both franchise and business.

I'm passionate about helping and supporting both new and existing teams in the games industry and you can see the types of services I can offer below.

I've built a wealth of experience and skills over the years including:

  - Studio Direction & Management
  - Leadership & People Management
  - Production Management
  - Game Direction
  - Business Development including Pitching & Contract Negotiation

I have a strong background in Games Production, having contributed to a number of studios and projects since 2011, including AAA and Indie, leading teams and developing titles across multiple platforms such as PC, Console, VR and Mobile. You can see just some of the games I've worked on below!

I'm also available for speaking opportunities and journalist contributions, and one of my talks even got a write up on gamesindustry.biz.
